## Readout

This random-1000 run supports the same conclusion as the random-50,
random-200, and first-1000 layers: `single_loc` is stable for exact rewrite and
teacher-forcing locality at scale, but it is not a paraphrase generalization
solution.

The random selection slightly improves rewrite over the first-1000 selection
while keeping locality nearly identical. PS@All remains weak under both
teacher-forcing and probability diagnostics. This is useful evidence for an
audited hotfix profile, not enough for a broad semantic-update or lifelong
editing claim.
